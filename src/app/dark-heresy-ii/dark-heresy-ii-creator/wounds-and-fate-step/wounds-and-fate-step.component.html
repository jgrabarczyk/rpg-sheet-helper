Wounds determine how much hit points your character has.

Base Wounds are tied to your Homeworld.

To determine Total Wounds roll d5 and add it to your homeworld's base wounds.

Fate determine how lucky is your character.

Base Fate is tied to your Homeworld.

To Determine Total Fate roll d10 and compare it to your homewolrd's blessingThreshold ({{homeworld.value.blessingThreshold}}). If you roll equal or higer add 1 to your Total Fate
